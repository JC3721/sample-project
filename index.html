Veni, Vidi, Vici

De longe veni, spatium et tempus formans ut in tuum regnum ingrediar tantum ut audiam te, dicis, "Puto id esse patrem Iuliani."

Venus, the unknown ruler and conqueror.
Lover of the night with a dreamer's heart.
Resilience leaves him unmatched,
Perseverance like no other.
Venus, the mighty warrior, a God.
Love conquers all.
